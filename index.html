About Us

Blu Fish Collaborative Inc. is a Landscape Architecture firm based in Austin, Texas. As a firm, we bring over 40 years of experience in excellent client service, creative design, and a collaborative approach to every project. With each project that we take on, we incorporate our values of collaboration, service, creativity, and fun - while creating spaces that bring out the natural areas’ flora and fauna. Our leadership and expertise include corporate office, commercial, retail, K-12 and higher education, multifamily, parks and recreation, and government institutions.
