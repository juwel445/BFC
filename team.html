LANDSCAPE ARCHITECTURE POSITION (5+ YEARS EXPERIENCE)

Blu Fish Collaborative is accepting resumes and portfolios for a landscape architect position requiring five to ten years of experience.

Requirements for candidates are a degree in Landscape Architecture, experience in a design firm and a passion for design and the profession of landscape architecture. Other necessary skills include: proficiency with AutoCAD, Microsoft Office programs (Word, Excel), Adobe Creative Suite programs (Photoshop, InDesign, Illustrator), Sketchup, hand drawing skills, and producing detailed and comprehensive construction documents. Salary and benefits are commensurate with experience and qualifications.

Blu Fish Collaborative has a wide range of experience. Our works include higher education, K-12, corporate office, commercial, community planning, parks & recreation, and multi-family. Blu Fish Collaborative is currently working in multiple states with the majority of its works located around the state of Texas.

LANDSCAPE ARCHITECTURE POSITION (0 TO 5 YEARS EXPERIENCE)

Blu Fish Collaborative is accepting resumes and portfolios for a landscape architect or landscape designer position requiring one to three years of experience.

Requirements for candidates are a degree in Landscape Architecture, experience in a design firm and a passion for design and the profession of landscape architecture. Other necessary skills include: proficiency with AutoCAD, Microsoft Office programs (Word, Excel), Adobe Creative Suite programs (Photoshop, InDesign, Illustrator), Sketchup, and hand drawing skills.

Blu Fish Collaborative is committed to furthering the profession of Landscape Architecture and encourages its employees to become licensed professionals. The individual hired for the position will work closely with the partners of the firm. The daily work experience will provide employees with the skills, experience and mentorship to become registered and to meet their professional goals. The expectations for the position include participation in the design process, preparation of presentation documents and assisting in the preparation of construction documents.

Blu Fish Collaborative has a wide range of experience. Our works include higher education, K-12, corporate office, commercial, community planning, parks & recreation, and multi-family. Blu Fish Collaborative is currently working in multiple states with the majority of its works located around the state of Texas.
